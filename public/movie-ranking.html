Before Sunrise (1995)

This film beautifully captures the magic of connecting with someone on a deep level,
igniting a fire of passion and excitement that will warm your heart. A must-watch
for anyone who believes in the power of true love!


Little Women (2019)

Experience the charm of this wonderful movie directed by the talented Greta Gerwig.
You'll find yourself feeling all sorts of emotions as you follow the characters
through their ups and downs of growing up.


Kimi no nawa (2016)

Your Name takes you on a journey of connection and destiny. It's a tender yet powerful
tale that reaffirms the existence of soulmates. Through its captivating storytelling,
the movie beautifully illustrates the true magic and kindness of genuine love.


Sociedad de la Nieve (2023)

From the real Los Andes tragedy of 1972, this movie walks you through all of the tremendous
events that this group of people had to survive. It's not an easy watch, but it's definitely
worth it. My respect to those who survived, and my heartfelt tribute to those who didn't.
